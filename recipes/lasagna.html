Lasagna

Description

This is a classic lasagna recipe that is hearty, filling, and perfect for family dinners. It features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and plenty of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce in a large skillet.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  3. Mix the ricotta cheese with the egg.
  4. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish: sauce, noodles, cheese, repeat.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 50 minutes.
  6. Let it cool before serving.
