Homemade Pizza

Description

Making homemade pizza dough is easier than you think. Top it with your favorite ingredients for a perfect movie night dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the sauce and add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden.
  5. Slice and enjoy!
