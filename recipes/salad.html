Greek Salad

Description

A fresh and healthy Greek salad with cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese. It is the perfect side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil and Oregano

Steps

  1. Chop the cucumbers and tomatoes into chunks.
  2. Slice the red onion thinly.
  3. Combine all vegetables in a large bowl.
  4. Add olives and crumbled feta cheese.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano. Toss gently.
